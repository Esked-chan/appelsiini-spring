About Us

The goal of our project is to build an ML/AI system that uses real-time sensor data and camera images to improve road safety and optimize travel routes.

We had a chance to work with "Marjetas" company in Rovaniemi once again this semester.

Our team will work with data from weather stations that measure road friction, water film thickness, air/surface temperatures, and humidity.
